About the Podcast

Peak Cinema is FPS's flagship podcast where our resident film experts Tony and Austin dive deep into the world of cinema. From classic Hollywood to modern indie films, we explore the art, craft, and culture that makes movies magical.

Each week, we bring you engaging discussions, insightful interviews with industry professionals, and passionate debates about everything from cinematography techniques to storytelling methods. Whether you're a film student or just a movie lover, Peak Cinema has something for everyone.
