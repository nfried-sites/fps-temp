About the Show

Don't Make Me Laugh is FPS's flagship sketch comedy production, featuring fast-paced, original skits created, shot, and edited entirely by our members. Each episode brings fresh humor and creative storytelling to the Binghamton community.

Our talented cast and crew work together to produce weekly episodes that cover everything from campus life to social commentary, all with a comedic twist. The show has become a staple of FPS programming and continues to grow in popularity.
